It's Never to Late to Discover the Power of Music
Sign up today!





Everyone has the potential to develop an outlet in music, and playing piano can help establish this foundation of love for music. Melissa has been a piano instructor for over 30 years. She offers private lessons to ensure each student learns in the manner that is best suited for them.
It's truly one of the best, lifelong gifts a parent can give to their child. Melissa recommends beginning piano lessons at about the age of 5. Students are able to choose from a wide variety of music styles. Piano recitals are optional, low-pressure, and offered throughout the year for all students. Whatever your age, it will be an unforgettable experience.
